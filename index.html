Privacy

Built for school trust and controlled data access.

This page summarizes NexioraAI's privacy posture for schools evaluating the product. Final commercial terms may include a school-specific data agreement.

Last updated: June 22, 2026

School-controlled data

NexioraAI is designed around school workspaces. Student records, academic materials, assessment activity, and user access stay scoped to the school account that owns them.

Role-based access

Users see information based on the school role and classroom responsibility assigned to them. Access is not inferred from email alone.

Operational security

Authentication, tenant boundaries, and internal service authentication are treated as core product requirements, not optional add-ons.

Principles

What schools should expect.

NexioraAI should not sell school or student data.

Sensitive school outputs should remain reviewable before they are shared.

Access should be explicit, role-aware, and removable by the school.

Production deployments should use configured secrets, approved domains, and secure service authentication.

Need school-specific privacy review?

We can share the operational details needed for procurement, data review, and school leadership approval during onboarding.

Terms

Product terms for responsible school use.

These terms describe the intended operating model for NexioraAI. A signed agreement may define additional commercial, legal, and data-processing terms for each school.

Last updated: June 22, 2026

School-authorized use

NexioraAI is intended for schools, school groups, and authorized education teams. Access should be granted by an authorized school or NexioraAI platform owner.

Human responsibility

AI assistance should support academic work, not replace professional judgment. Schools remain responsible for final decisions, communication, and policy alignment.

Account access

Users are responsible for signing in with the account invited by their school. Access can be changed or removed when roles, classes, or school responsibilities change.

Operating expectations

Clear boundaries before scale.

Schools should configure roles, classes, and access before inviting users.

Users should only access records connected to their assigned responsibility.

Sensitive outputs should be reviewed before being sent to students, families, or leadership.

NexioraAI may suspend access if usage creates security, privacy, or operational risk.
