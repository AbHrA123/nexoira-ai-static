What you get

A full assessment & learning loop — owned by your school.

From the moment a paper is scanned to the moment a student finishes their personalised practice quiz, every step lives inside your school's bubble. We build the tooling; the data stays yours.

Scan from anywhere

ADF document scanners, phone cameras, multi-function printers, shared drives — feed papers in however your school already works. Your scans stay yours; nothing trains our models, nothing leaves your bucket.

  • Auto-document feeders (ADF)
  • Phone & tablet cameras
  • Existing scanner uploads
  • Custom source? Just ask.

AI-assisted grading

Marking-scheme-aware suggestions per question with a step-by-step breakdown. Teachers approve, edit, or override every mark before students see anything.

Class-wise insights & focus points

Topic-grouped weak-area heatmaps for each class and each test, a principal-ready Excel workbook updated daily with a Daily History tab, and at-risk-student call-outs the teacher can act on in one click.

AI tutor that knows your books

Every student gets a private tutor grounded in your school's own textbooks, notes, and previous papers. Every answer cites the page it came from — no hallucinations, no random internet content, no model training on your material.

Personalised quizzes for every student

After every paper, NexioraAI auto-generates a custom quiz for each student — drawn from their weak topics, aligned to your syllabus, calibrated to the questions they actually got wrong. Practice exactly where it improves them, refreshed automatically.

Your data stays yours

Scans, marks, student chats, teacher notes — all of it lives in your school's storage bucket. We never train models on it, never sell it, never share it. Export the full dataset at any time, or revoke our access in one click.

It's your school. It's your data.

No third-party resale, no lock-in. Storage lives in the school's own GCP bucket, and every student chat / mark / answer sheet is exportable on demand.
